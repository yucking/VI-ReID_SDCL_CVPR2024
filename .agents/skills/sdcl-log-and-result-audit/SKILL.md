# SDCL Log And Result Audit

Only cite real log content. Do not infer experiment behavior from filenames.

## Required Checks

- Training completed or stopped early.
- Monitor actually ran and did not exit prematurely.
- `summary.md` exists and was generated by the parser.
- Best model epoch versus final epoch.
- all-search versus indoor-search.
- Rank-1, mAP, and mINP separately.
- Single trial versus 10-trial average.
- Baseline repeated or not.
- Random fluctuation estimate if repeated baseline exists.
- Checkpoint existence: `checkpoint.pth.tar`, `model_best.pth.tar`, `20model_best.pth.tar`.
- Error patterns: traceback, CUDA OOM, NaN, RuntimeError, and loss explosion.
- Selective reporting risk.

## Outputs

Produce both:

- Human summary with cited log paths and lines or exact snippets.
- Machine-readable row suitable for `experiments/registry.csv`.

Use `UNKNOWN` when evidence is absent and `UNVERIFIED` when a claim exists without raw evidence.
